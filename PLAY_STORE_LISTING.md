## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Plural Space is a private, offline system management app built for plural individuals — those with DID, OSDD, or any form of plurality.

With Simply Plural ending development, the plural community deserves a capable, privacy-first alternative that puts your system's data entirely in your hands.

**What Plural Space does:**

◈ FRONT TRACKING
Know who's currently fronting at a glance. Set single or co-fronts, add context notes, and see how long the current front has been active — all updated in real time.

◇ MEMBER REGISTRY
Keep a full profile for every headmate: name, pronouns, role or archetype, a personal color, and a bio. Search and filter as your system grows.

◷ FRONT HISTORY
Every front change is logged automatically with timestamps and duration. Grouped by date so patterns are easy to spot.

◉ SYSTEM JOURNAL
Write entries, tag which members were present or involved, and keep a running record of your system's inner life.

↑ EXPORT & SHARE
Your data stays on your device. When you want to share it:
— Export as JSON (importable back in, full backup)
— Export as HTML (opens directly in Google Docs when uploaded to Drive)
— Send a formatted summary via your email app

**Privacy first, always.**
Plural Space stores everything locally on your device. No accounts. No servers. No analytics. No internet required. Your system's data belongs to your system.

**Built by a plural system, for the plural community.**
Plural Space is developed and maintained by The Hanyou System. We understand what this kind of tool needs to do because we use it ourselves.
